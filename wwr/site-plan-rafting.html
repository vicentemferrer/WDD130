Overview

Purpose

We need this website to reach and generate more potential customers for our company.
We'll accomplish this by share experiences of our rafting groups and spread information
about rafting. This will benefit our company creating confidence with our posts, what
will increase our incomes gradually.

Audience

Our target audience are young adults between 25 and 35 years, middle class students and employees.
They may be single or married. Their interests are physical activities, social media, outdoors outings,
hiking, travel, sports, and meet places and people.

They want vacations, have a break of their routine, overcome challenges, be free of their duties.
They have needs more related to Social, Esteem, and Self-Actualization hierarchy levels. Why they will
acquire our service is by a personal seeking of adrenaline, challenges, and risks; looking for disconnect
from the world; and share with who they love.

Our website will be accessed by desktop, laptop, and mobile devices, being the last more usually used.
