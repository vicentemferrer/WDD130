Overview

Purpose

We need this website to reach and generate potential customers for our enterpreneurship. We'll accomplish this by explaining what we do, sharing the projects in which we are involved and providing contact information for who are interested in provide outsourcing to their own projects and business. This will benefit our company creating confidence by proving our experience in the field, what will increase our recommendations and projects participation average.

Audience

Our target audience are entrepreneurs and small and medium businesses. They may have some years of trajectory in their sector, and they are ready to consider a national or international expansion. They are interested on a established logistic chain ready to use and a control structure integrated and offered as a external service to manage importations, exportations, and resource transportations. Their needs are related to Security hierarchy level. They want trust and security in their intermediate operations, flexibility, and operability. They seek a service who may take care about their needs and help them to reach a higher profitability. That's why they will use our services. Our website will be accessed by desktop, laptop, and mobile devices, being the large ones more usually used.
